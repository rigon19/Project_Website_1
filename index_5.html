    • Vasily Lomachenko

    Life

    Born on February 17th 1988 in Odesa Ukraine. Having a father that he himself was a boxer he began his journey since a baby.

    Career

    Having the most succesful amateur career with 396 wins and 1 loss the sport has seen, his professional debut was long awaited. He won his first title on his third professional fight, second on his 7th and 3rd on his 11th. This made him the fastest boxer to ever earn a title.

    Record

    His record stands at : 19 total fights, 17 wins, 2 losses, 0 draws with 11 KO's
